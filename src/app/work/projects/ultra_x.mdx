## Project Overview
Ultra X needed to migrate from their existing WooCommerce platform to Shopify to achieve faster performance and better scalability as their business grew.

## What I Delivered
- Complete migration from WooCommerce to Shopify without data loss.
- Optimized theme for faster page loads and improved conversion rates.
- Implemented scalable architecture to support growing product catalog and traffic.
- Ensured SEO preservation during the migration process.

## Outcome
The new Shopify store delivers significantly faster page load times and provides Ultra X with a platform that can scale seamlessly with their continued growth.
