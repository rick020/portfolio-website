## Project Overview
At The Table needed a website that reflected the creativity and quality of the brand. The existing site did not fully showcase the visual identity or guide visitors clearly through the shopping experience.

I designed and built a completely new Shopify storefront, introducing a stronger brand system, custom illustrations, and a more engaging visual narrative that mirrors the aesthetic of the tablescapes themselves.

## What I Delivered
- Designed and built a **fully custom Shopify storefront** from the ground up.
- Created a **new visual direction for the brand**, including a set of custom illustrations used throughout the site.
- Developed **flexible Shopify sections** so the team can easily update pages without developer support.

## Outcome
The new site gives At The Table a digital presence that better reflects the brand’s personality and creativity.
