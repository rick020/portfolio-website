## Project Overview
Clementine Parker required a polished ecommerce experience that reflected the brand while staying performant across mobile and desktop.

## What I Delivered
- Developed and configured a Shopify storefront with a strong visual hierarchy.
- Optimized layout and asset usage to keep pages lightweight.
- Structured content blocks so the brand team could run launches and seasonal updates independently.

## Outcome
The final build improved the overall customer journey, reduced friction in product browsing, and created a maintainable platform for future campaigns.
